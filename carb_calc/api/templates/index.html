About the Project

The Carbon Calculator project aims to raise awareness about carbon emissions and help individuals estimate their carbon footprint. By analyzing video footage, the system can identify and track sources of carbon emissions, providing valuable insights into environmental impact.

Through this project, we hope to empower individuals to make more informed choices about their daily activities and encourage sustainable practices to reduce carbon emissions and combat climate change.
